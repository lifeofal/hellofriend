About me

Hello Friend! I'm Alejandro Cervantes, born May 30th 1997. I am a Mexican American with a Bachelor of Science Degree in Computer Science. I graduated in December 2021. I am fluent in both Spanish and English. I am always ready for a challange which is why I found the programming world so interesting. I got into coding after facing a difficult problem in my every day life. Back when instagram what a big chunk in my social life, I disliked the type of people that would follow me and unfollow me once I followed them back. This 'Follow for Follow' farming got on my nerves so I downloaded an app on my phone that could track unfollowers. Eventually, Instagram shut down their API program which rendered alot of these apps useless and riddles with bugs. After reseaching more about how one could retrieve data from a browser, I came across a Java package called Selenium. This was the birth of my InstaInfo project. A Instagram bot that can log into your account and compair your followers and following and return a list of non-followers.
